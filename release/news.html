News

Version 0.7.0 released:

New functions in this release include the ability to import CSV-files as simulation slaves (with linear interpolation of tabulated values). Also new is the ability to output internal variables of slaves, and recognize and filter synonymous variables. See details in the ChangeLog and the Modelica-Newsletter.
